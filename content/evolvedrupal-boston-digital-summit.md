DubBot is proud to sponsor this outstanding event where agency leaders, technologists, creatives, and higher education professionals converge to discuss accessibility, digital strategy, content, and user experience.

DubBot’s own [Penny Kronz](https://www.linkedin.com/in/penny-kronz-7046969/ "This link leaves the DubBlog website.") will share her knowledge and expertise in her session "ABCs of Website Accessibility." In it, she will cover web accessibility terminology in a simple, approachable format.

This one-day summit combines the best of technology, design, strategy, AI, and education while covering a range of topics including coding, UX/UI design, digital advancements, web accessibility, and more. 

EvolveDrupal Boston Digital Summit takes place on Friday, June 6, starting at 9 a.m. at the [Microsoft New England Research and Development (NERD) Center](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-new-england/ "This link leaves the DubBlog website."), 1 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA.
