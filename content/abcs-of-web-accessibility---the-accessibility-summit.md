**Digital Collegium runs on volunteer power and the invaluable support of our sponsors!** DubBot is proud to continue our sponsorship this year as a sponsor for the [Accessibility Summit](https://events.digicol.org/a11ysummit25 "This link leaves the DubBlog website.") on Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

The [Accessibility Summit](https://events.digicol.org/a11ysummit25 "This link leaves the DubBlog website.") is a one-day, multi-track, all-virtual event focused on digital accessibility in higher education.

This is your chance to dive into digital accessibility best practices and connect with peers from colleges and universities across the country. Whether you’re just getting started or deep into the weeds of WCAG, you'll find sessions and conversations that spark ideas and will help level up your accessibility game. Come ready to learn, share, and grow alongside a passionate community working to make the web more inclusive for all.

And you won’t want to miss DubBot’s own [Penny Kronz](https://www.linkedin.com/in/penny-kronz-7046969/ "This link leaves the DubBlog website."), who will share her knowledge and expertise in her session, [**ABCs and 1,2,3s of Website Accessibility**](https://events.digicol.org/a11ysummit25/session/3213278/abcs-and-123s-of-website-accessibility "This link leaves the DubBlog website."). In it, she will cover web accessibility terminology in a simple, approachable format. You don’t want to miss this session!

Mark your calendars and [register now for the Accessibility Summit 2025](https://events.digicol.org/a11ysummit25/registration "This link leaves the DubBlog website.")! 

And while you’re checking your calendar, don’t forget to reserve September 28 - October 1, 2025, for the [Digital Collegium Annual Conference](https://events.digicol.org/digicol25/ "This link leaves the DubBlog website.") - **the premier gathering for higher education professionals passionate about digital innovation**.
